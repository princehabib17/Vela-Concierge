Tools as entry points, not features.

Scarcity. One CTA. No noise.
The home page closes with the most important thing — a single call to action and a scarcity signal. "Paul is accepting commissions" tells you there's a real person on the other end. "Limited to 12 commissions per year" makes the opportunity feel rare without screaming FOMO. One button. That's it.
